The 20th International Conference on
Artificial Intelligence and Statistics

Apr 20-21, 2017
Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA

The 20th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics (AISTATS 2017) will be held in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA.  Since its inception in 1985, AISTATS has been an interdisciplinary gathering of researchers at the intersection of computer science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, and related areas.  The deadline for submission of papers this year is Oct 13, 2016, more details can be found at the call for papers.
