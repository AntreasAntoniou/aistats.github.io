The 19th International Conference on
Artificial Intelligence and Statistics

May 9 - 11, 2016
Cadiz, Spain

The 19th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics (AISTATS 2016) will be held in Cadiz, Spain.  AISTATS is an interdisciplinary gathering of researchers at the intersection of computer science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, and related areas.  Since its inception in 1985, the primary goal of AISTATS has been to broaden research in these fields by promoting the exchange of ideas among them.  We encourage the submission of all papers which are in keeping with this objective.
