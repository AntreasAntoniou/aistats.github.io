Visa

In case you need to apply for a visa to enter the United States for AISTATS 2017, please contact Karen Smith at aistats2017.logistics@gmail.com. Please put "AISTATS invitation letter" in the subject line of your email, with the following information in the contents:
  • Name (as it appears on your passport)
  • Affiliation/Address
  • Date of birth
  • Place of Birth (City, Country)
